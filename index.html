Who Is Shiva Infratech?

Started in 2015, we specialize in Civil & Structural Construction of industrial foundations, structures, and plants. Shiva Infratech is a locally owned Indian Infrastructure company dedicated to providing comprehensive Engineering Construction and Project Management services.

From town maintenance work to fast-growing piling works, we maintain a strong and well-trained local workforce guided by able management.

Our Vision

A regional market leader in provision of civil engineering, Infrastructure and building construction services.

Our Mission

To emerge as a world-class construction company, offering efficient, affordable, sustainable engineering solutions.
